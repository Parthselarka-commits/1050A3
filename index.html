About Comic Universe

Comic Universe was founded in 2015 by a group of passionate collectors and has grown to become Melbourne's premier destination for comics, manga, and pop culture collectibles. Our mission is to create a welcoming community space for fans of all ages to discover new stories and connect with fellow enthusiasts.
